test_maintain_mic_state_when_anacapa_stops(**kwargs)[source]¶

Tests that the mic state is maintained if anacapa is stopped and restarted. Also tests that mics mute, when anacapa shuts down, and they cannot be unmuted from the sig detail. Steps: 2) Stop anacapa 3) Verify that mics have muted 4) Verify that mics can not be re-enabled 5) Restart anacapa 6) Verify that mics return to state before anacapa stopped 7) Mute mics and repeat steps 2-6

Parameters:zp (:class:`~sonos.client.internal.SonosZoneComponent) – The zone player under test
test_mic_mutes_with_no_voice_account(**kwargs)[source]¶

Tests that the mics remain muted when there is no voice account enabled on the DUT Steps: 2) Remove the voice account 3) Verify that mics are muted 4) Verify that mics cannot be enabled 5) Configure a voice account 6) Verify that mics can be enabled
