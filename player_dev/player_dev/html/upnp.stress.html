test_delegation_to_ht_master(**kwargs)[source]¶

Test delegation to ht_master when ht_master is a group member and has TV content to play. The original GC should delegate to ht_master and becomes GM of the new group whose GC is ht_master playing TV Case #1: Auto Play : ON

  1. Start music playback on GC that is not ht_master
  2. Group all members
  3. Verify all grouped and playing music
  4. Start HDMI playback
  5. Verify delegation: ht_master as GC all others as GMs
  6. Verify all playing TV
Case #2: Auto Play : OFF
1, 2, 3, 4 : as above 5. Verify no delegation. 6. Turn auto play 7. Verify as in 5 and 6 of case 1
Case #3: Auto Play : OFF
1, 2,3, 4 : as above 5 : as in case 2 6. SetAVTransport URI on non ht_master GC to that of TV 7. Verify as in 5 and 6 of case 1
