class upnp.stress.robustness.verify_play_with_latency.VerifyPlayWithLatency(fixture_name=None, *args, **kwargs)[source]¶

Bases: sonos.workflow.fixture.WorkflowTestFixture

Under high latency conditions during playback, it was observed that the player would enter a state where audio playback was continuing but no audio was coming out of the speakers. The catastrophic message the player spits out should not be encountered even with transient high latency. This verifies SWPBL-57084 - no audio after high latency

Start playback, increase delay to a random value and monitor for the diagnostic that indicates the CHSNK module has stopped. If we ever get the diagnostic, fail the test. In case the delay causes the player to stop playback, reduce the delay and start playback again.
