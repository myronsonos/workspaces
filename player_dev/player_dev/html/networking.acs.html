test_radar_flee_time_with_sats(**kwargs)[source]¶

Verifies that when a radar event is detected by HT with satellites that the primary and satellites stop transmitting data frames within 200 ms and leave the channel within 10 seconds. During that 10 seconds, after the initial 200 ms, only management frames with an aggregate transmission time of 60 ms may be sent. This test does not verify such details and instead sets the pass criteria at a channel switch at less than 200 ms. This is because if we do utilize the time post 200 ms all satellites would drop out until going to a new channel.

Parameters:ht_master – the zone to test

verify_ht_quickly_flees_radar(ht_master, sats, dfs_channel)[source]¶

Verifies that zones which are on a DFS channel leave quickly in response to a radar event. The test is rather simplistic only verifying our leave channel time is less than the allowed data bucket interval of 200 ms. Compliance does more minute timing test for regulatory so we are just making sure we leave pretty quickly with master being the last to leave

Note

  • zones must leave the channel by 10 seconds
  • zones must stop transmitting data frames within 200 ms of the event
  • zones must not transmit for more than 60 ms worth of management frames within the 10 seconds after the initial radar event
Parameters:
  • ht_master (SonosZoneComponent) – the primary zone to check ht for post radar dwell time
  • sats (:obj:'list') – list of the satellites of primary which must flee before primary
  • dfs_channel – wireless DFS channel verify zones flee time against

trigger_lm_interval(master, interval)[source]¶

Method to cause the LM blacklist to trigger by disabling satellites every second, this method does not guarantee a LM blacklist so it will be called several times by the test method. The CM value prior to the change does not account for the random CM bias change of 63 that is applied when the channel change actaully happens. This means the best CM channel before the change may not be the best CM channel after the channel change.
