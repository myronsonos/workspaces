ZoneGroupTopology Zone Group Attribute state variables: * ZoneGroupName * ZoneGroupID * ZonePlayerUUIDsInGroup

Tests grouping positive tests * before grouping two ZPs, their group names are the same as their

(different) zone names, their group IDs are different, and their group UUID lists each have only their respective UUID
  • after grouping, the group name on both is the coordinator’s zone name suffixed with ” + 1”, both have the same group ID, and both have the same list of UUIDs, in which the coordinator is 1st
  • after ungrouping, the group IDs are different again

stereo pair positive tests * in a stereo pair, the group name on both devices is the left

(primary) channel’s name, the group ID is the same on both devices, and the UUID list on both devices is just the left channel’s UUID
  • after unlinking the pair, the group names, group ID, and UUID lists are different on the two devices

ungroupable devices (one groupable and one dock) * group IDs are different before attempting to group * grouping fails * group IDs are still different after bridges and docks * group name, group ID and group UUIDs variables are not evented
