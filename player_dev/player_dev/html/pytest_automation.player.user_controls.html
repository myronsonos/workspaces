pytest_automation.player.user_controls.test_crossfade module¶

This test module aims at testing the crossfade functionality on the players by analyzing the audio output of the players’ DSPOUT audiotaps.

The core of the test executes by queueing up 2 tracks in the players’ queue. One of the tracks has a sine tone playing at one frequency. The other track has a sine tone playing at a different frequency. Each of the tracks is > 30s in durations, so as to provide enough audio for crossfade to execute.

The tracks are played back with and without crossfade enabled. The analysis involves taking FFT windows throughout time slices of the entire audiotap duration, which should be around 1 minute without crossfade enabled and around 45s with crossfade enabled (15s of first, 15s of both, 15s of second).

The FFTs without crossfade should only one window where both tones are present in the frequency spectrum of the audio. The FFTs with crossfade should show 15s of the first tone, 15s where the first tone progressively attenuates and the second tone progressively increments, and 15s of the second tone.
