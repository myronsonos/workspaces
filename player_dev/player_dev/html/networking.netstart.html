Verifies that the satellites of the given ht config have the same operating channel as the HT master’s Home Theater (HT) channel within 10 seconds or fails the test.

Note

This method was used instead of a simple assert due to DFS on 5GHz bands. 10 seconds was chosen because it is the MAX change time defined by DFS requirements. For CAC channel clear may take up to 60 seconds…

Verifies…

  1. Legacy devices that support v2 certs and have them respond with extra metadata their cert is signed with
  2. Legacy devices that support v2 certs and do not have them do not respond with extra metadata
  3. Legacy devices that do not support v2 certs do not respond with extra metadata
  4. Secure boot devices respond with the extra metadata their cert is signed with
